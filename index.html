Budapest, Normafa Park
Jánoshegyi út, 1121

Normafa is part of the Buda Hills area, on the Svábhegy (Schwab Hill), close to János-hegy (Janos Hill), the highest point of the city. It's known for its panoramic scenery and fresh air.

Hungarian: Szentendre
Fő tér 5, 2000

Szentendre is a Hungarian town on the Danube River, north of the capital, Budapest. It’s known for its baroque architecture, churches, colorful houses, cobbled streets.
